Project Vikram

Vikram is my Level 1 Tripoli High-Powered Rocketry certification rocket. Named after Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, this rocket features a hand-fabricated body tube, plywood fins, reinforced motor mount, and custom avionics bay. The rocket reached an apogee of 2304 ft and a peak velocity of 572 ft/s, achieving a successful recovery and certification.
