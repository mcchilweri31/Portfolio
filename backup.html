Project Vikram

Vikram is my Level 1 High-Powered Rocketry certification rocket. Named after Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, it features hand-fabricated structural parts and a reliable recovery system. The flight reached 2304 ft and achieved full mission success.

NASA USLI: Project NORMAN

This marks the first time any team from the state of Oklahoma has participated in the NASA Student Launch competition.

Project NORMAN was the University of Oklahoma’s entry into the 2024–2025 NASA Student Launch Initiative. As the Aerodynamics Lead on the Boomer Rocket Team, I was responsible for CFD simulations, flight stability, drag optimization, and integration with the payload bay and recovery system. Our team developed a robust, reusable vehicle that achieved a successful launch at Huntsville, Alabama on May 4th 2025.
